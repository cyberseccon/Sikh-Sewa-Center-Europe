Our Mission

Rooted in Seva,
Serving All of Humanity

Sikh Sewa Center Europe was established to unite the Sikh diaspora, preserve our luminous heritage, and serve all of humanity — regardless of faith, background, or nationality.

Inspired by the teachings of the Gurus, we embody Seva (selfless service), Simran (meditation on the divine), and Sangat (holy community) in every action we take.

🕌

Religious Services

Kirtan, Akhand Path, and spiritual events connecting Gurdwaras across Europe.

📚

Education & Learning

Gurbani, Sikh history, Punjabi language, and youth development programmes.

🤝

Interfaith Harmony

Building bridges through shared human values of compassion and equality.

About Sikh Sewa Center Europe

Serving with love, faith, and unity across the continent since 2010.

Our Story

A Home for Every Sikh in Europe

Founded in 2010 by a group of dedicated Sikh families across the UK, Germany, and the Netherlands, Sikh Sewa Center Europe was born from one profound vision: that no Sikh in Europe should ever feel spiritually alone.

Over fifteen years, we have grown into a continent-wide movement — organising Kirtan evenings, Langar days, youth camps, interfaith forums, and educational programmes across 20+ European countries.

Our doors are open to all — Sikhs, those learning about Sikhi, and everyone who seeks community, spiritual nourishment, and the gift of Langar.
